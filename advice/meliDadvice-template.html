Hi my name is Melissa and I have some advice for you!

Social Media is your friend!

When you are just starting out, you'll have a ton of questions.

Find an online community of other learners! There are many great groups of people on Social Media who were just like you and now they'd love to help!

You are not an imposter, you are learning!

It can feel like you'll never become a developer, but trust me, we've all been there.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You see when you encounter a new programming language part of your learning process includes interacting with other developers. No matter how long you've been learning or working, problems will arise that you need someone else to help fix. Yes, you can google your way through, and that's a great place to start. In the end though, it is helpful to have someone to talk to and help work through a problem.
